RollMazer

Project Description

RollMazer is a ball-in-a-maze game. The player navigates the ball through a maze by tilting the whole playing field while avoiding the holes in the maze. The difficulty is set by the size of the maze and comes with three difficulty levels. To bring variety to the game the maze is generated.
