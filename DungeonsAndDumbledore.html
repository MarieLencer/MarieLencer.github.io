Dungeons & Dumbledore

Project Description

Dungeons & Dumbledore is a rogue-like game created with Unity. Taking inspiration from Coldmirror's version of Fresh Dumbledore, the player explores Dumbledore's adventures while running through various dungeons. The player can encounter three types of enemies and receive both temporary buffs as well as permanent buffs by defeating the enemies. At the end of the dungeon a final boss awaits the player.
