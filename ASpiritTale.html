A Spirit Tale

Project Description/h2>

A Spirit Tale is a VR fitness game developed for the Oculus Quest through the usage of Unity. The player will encounter the spirits of three lost places. To defeat the spirits the player will have to use boxing, posing and drawing. With each defeated enemy the monochrome world will gain a bit of colour again. Each place has a story to tell and a final boss to encounter.
