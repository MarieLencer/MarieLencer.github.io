4 Elements

Project Description

4 Elements is a multiplayer for up to four players playing together on one computer with each character representing one of the four elements: water, fire, earth and air. Using the latest features from Unity and Nvidia to present a high quality playing field with real time raytracing. The players have to work together to defeat the evergrowing waves of spawning enemies. An enemy AI was created to determine the enemy behaviour and movement through a behaviour tree and navigation system. The playing field is generated to created more diverse playing experience.
