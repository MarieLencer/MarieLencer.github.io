Spirit Garden

Project Description

Spirit Garden is a adventure game meets virtual pet game. Players can explore a magic garden filled with mysterious creatures. The goal of the game is to get to know these creaures and befriend them. With their help new areas can be explored and new creatures met. To gain the trust and friendship of creature their mannerisms, preferences and dislikes should be observed while their needs have to be met. Players can document new insights, like the hiding spots of a particular creature, the recipe to their favorite food or images of a new creature, in a dedicated journal.

This project was created as a semester project of my game design master's degree at the Hochschule für Technik und Wirtschaft Berlin.

My Responsibilites

This game was developed by Bianca Dreißig, Connor Peters, Finn Teichler and me. My responsibilites in the project where the game design and project management as well as the programming of certain gameplay features. These features include:

  • Creature behaviour through the implementation of a behaviour tree
    • Walking around
    • Eating
    • Sleeping
    • Following the player
  • Journal
  • Main Menu
All assets in the game where either obtained as free assets from the Unity Asset Store or created by the team.
